1. Open VSCode.
2. Open the command palette:
   - `Ctrl` + `Shift` + `P` (Windows/Linux)
   - `Cmd` + `Shift` + `P` (Mac)
3. Type and select `File: Open Recent`.
4. A list of recently opened projects and files will appear. Click on the desired project to open it.
