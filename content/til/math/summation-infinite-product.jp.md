**総和:**

- $ \sum\_{i=0}^{N} x_i = x_0 + x_1 + x_2 + ... + x_N $
- LaTeXコマンド: `\sum_{i=0}^{N} x_i = x_0 + x_1 + x_2 + ... + x_N`

**無限積:**

- $ \prod\_{i=0}^{N} x_i = x_0 \times x_1 \times x_2 \times ... \times x_N $
- LaTeXコマンド: `\prod_{i=0}^{N} x_i = x_0 \times x_1 \times x_2 \times ... \times x_N`
